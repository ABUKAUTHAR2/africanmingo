5 DAYS, 4 NIGHTS SAFARI IN TANZANIA

Tarangire, Lake Manyara, and Ngorongoro Crater

Safari Overview: Expect to be impressed by the natural beauty of Africa’s most famous park. Explore Tanzania’s iconic national parks Tarangire and Lake Manyara on this 5-day Safari. Start in Arusha before experiencing a week of incredible safari...

DAY ONE. Arrival: Welcome to Tanzania, Your African Flamingo Tour and Safari guide will meet you upon arrival at Kilimanjaro Airport and take you straight to your lodge in Arusha. This trip is an exclusive private safari so that you can enjoy your trip to the fullest. Tomorrow morning will be an early start for your first Tanzania safari experience, so use your first day to build up your strength from a day of travel and immerse in the sights and sounds of Africa. Don’t be afraid to ask your guide questions and tell them your needs and expectations. Decide your departure time and return time together and discuss specific animals you want to look out for. Enjoy all the nearby parks.

DAY TWO. Arusha to Tarangire National Park for Game Viewing: After breakfast, you will be picked up at your accommodation by our African Flamingo Tour and Safari guide and drive you to Tarangire National Park to get an experience and unforgettable memories in Tanzania wildlife safaris. Tarangire National Park is well known for its elephant families, which can often be seen congregating nearby the river. You may also see a large number of Giraffe, Bushbuck, and Wildebeest. These animals are closely followed by a range of predators including lions and leopards. Moreover, there are more breeding species of birds found in Tarangire National Park than anywhere else on the planet. Tarangire is the best place to see all these gentle giants in their natural habitat from the adorable calves all the way up to the threatening bulls. The park is also covered with big majestic baobab trees which are seen against the blue sky. (Read more about Tarangire) After lunch and an extensive game drive, we head to Bougainvillea lodge in Karatu for relaxing, Dinner, and Overnight.

DAY THREE. Tarangire to Lake-Manyara National Park: Early morning after breakfast, you will be heading out to your second Tanzania National Park, the magnificent Lake Manyara. Though one of the smaller national parks in the country, it still packs a punch. Expect to spot herds of elephants slowly trekking across the plains, vast troops of baboons in the thick forest canopy, and if you are lucky perhaps even tree climbing lions. You will spend the day in your private 4x4 safari vehicle with your experienced safari guide leading the way. A mesmerizing variety of wildlife can be found around the huge soda lake, including buffalos, warthogs, giraffes, wildebeests, zebras, and plenty of flamingos. The shallow hippo pool is always a highlight and offers excellent opportunities for hippo sightings as well as spectacular views of the Rift Valley Escarpment. Lake Manyara is not about epic game drives; however, combine a full day of safari adventure with various activities from cultural tours to canopy walks and more. After Lunch and an extensive game drive, we head to Tortilis lodge for relaxing, dinner, and overnight.

DAY FOUR. Ngorongoro Crater: After an early breakfast, we drive to Ngorongoro Crater for a game viewing drive. The Ngorongoro Crater is one of Africa’s most famous sites and is said to have the highest density of wildlife in Africa. Sometimes described as an eighth wonder of the world, the crater has achieved world renown, attracting an ever-increasing number of visitors each year. You are unlikely to escape other vehicles here, but you are guaranteed great viewing of wildlife in a genuinely mind-blowing environment. There is nowhere else in Africa quite like Ngorongoro; it is one of the best places in the country to see endangered black rhinoceros, lions, herds of wildebeest, zebras, gazelles, hippos, and giraffe. The park is home to the whole big five and is a particularly good place to spot lions and cape buffalos year-round. A hot packed lunch will be served at Ngorongoro picnic site, late afternoon we drive back to Arusha. Dinner and overnight will be in Arusha Town at Planet lodge.

DAY FIVE. Departure to Zanzibar/Home: After breakfast, you will be picked up by your private safari guide from your lodge and drive you to town for the last-minute shopping and repacking lunch. Transfer to the airport to catch your flight back home or a flight to Zanzibar.

5 DAYS, 4 NIGHTS SAFARI IN TANZANIA

Serengeti and Ngorongoro Crater

Safari Overview: Explore Africa’s greatest national parks on this 5-day Tanzania Safari. Start in Arusha before experiencing a week of incredible safari, including visiting two of Africa’s seven Natural Wonders: Serengeti National Park, home to an abundance of wildlife and the unforgettable Great Wildebeest Migration, and Ngorongoro Crater, a home of Big five animals and Cradle of humankind.

DAY ONE. Arrival: Welcome to Tanzania, Your African Flamingo Tour and Safari guide will meet you upon arrival at Kilimanjaro Airport and take you straight to your lodge in Arusha. This trip is an exclusive private safari so that you can enjoy your trip to the fullest. Tomorrow morning will be an early start for your first Tanzania safari experience, so use your first day to build up your strength from a day of travel and immerse in the sights and sounds of Africa. Don’t be afraid to ask your guide questions and tell them your needs and expectations. Decide your departure time and return time together and discuss specific animals you want to look out for. Enjoy all the nearby parks.

DAY TWO. Arusha to Serengeti National Park: After an early breakfast, our African Flamingo Tour and Safari Guide will pick you up at your hotel and drive you to Serengeti National Park. The Serengeti is truly spectacular; it is famous for its vast open grasslands which allow for excellent sightings of wildlife. The Serengeti is Tanzania’s oldest National Park and it is also one of the oldest ecosystems on earth. Its climate and vegetation have remained largely untouched for millions of years. At the same time, as the Serengeti boasts abundant wildlife, it is perhaps best known for the annual Great Migration. Vast numbers of wildebeest, accompanied by large numbers of zebra and antelope, move in an annual pattern, constantly seeking fresh grazing. The precise timing of the Migration is entirely dependent upon the rainfall patterns each year. When we arrive in Serengeti park we shall proceed with en route game driving and game viewing towards Central Serengeti for the whole afternoon up to late evening, where dinner and overnight will be at ……… tented camp in Central Serengeti National Park.

DAY THREE. Serengeti Park to Ngorongoro Crater: In day three, early morning after breakfast, you shall continue with game driving together with your private safari guide, looking for the herds across the plains. Apart from the migratory animals, you will also get to see lots of other animals like lions, cheetahs, leopards, and many more. Expect to be impressed by the natural beauty of Africa’s most famous park. After a picnic lunch in Serengeti Park, we drive for an exit game driving through the endless plains of Serengeti and heading to Ngorongoro Conservation Area for Dinner and overnight at Tortilis Tented Camp, while waiting for Crater game driving the coming day.

DAY FOUR. Ngorongoro Crater to Arusha Town: After waking up and having breakfast, we drive to Ngorongoro Conservation Area for game driving and game viewing into the Ngorongoro Crater. Ngorongoro is one of the famous world heritage sites in Tanzania, particularly in Africa and the World, with a unique natural wonder with the world's highest concentration of wildlife. When you arrive at the edge of Ngorongoro Crater, your eyes are welcomed by breathtaking views of the intact volcanic caldera and home to the highest density of Big game in Africa (Rhinos, Lions, Leopards, Buffalos, and Elephants). When you drive down into the Crater, it is an adventure in itself, and when you reach the Crater floor, you quickly find yourself among a large number of wildebeest, zebra, gazelle, and more than 500 bird species. The crater provides you with the best chance of seeing endangered black rhino, which with luck can be spotted in open grasslands (Read more about Ngorongoro Conservation Area) After a picnic lunch at Ngorongoro, we ascend the edges of the crater and drive back to Arusha Town for dinner and overnight at Planet Lodge, while waiting for your departure day.

DAY FIVE. Departure to Home or Zanzibar: After breakfast, you will be picked up by your private safari guide from your lodge and drive you to town for the last-minute shopping and repacking lunch. Transfer to the airport to catch your flight back home or a flight to Zanzibar.

ZANZIBAR PACKAGE 1

Day 1: ARRIVAL

Arrive at Zanzibar airport/ferry and transfer to the hotel for check-in and overnight.

Day 2: STONE TOWN + PRISON ISLAND + SPICE TOUR

At 9:00 am, the driver/guide will take you on a Stone Town Tour, exploring the historical culture and architecturally important town of Zanzibar with a century of history. The tour includes a visit to the former slave market site, now standing as the Anglican Church built in 1871, town market, Zanzibar Memorial Museum, Old Arab Fort, Palace Museum, Old Indian dispensary (Cultural Center), and Maruhubi Palace Ruins via Living Stone House.

After the Stone Town tour, the guide will take you to meet the boat captain heading to Prison Island, home of Aldabra giant tortoises. Flanked by an amazing beach of the reef sanctuary, the island is ideal for swimming, snorkeling, and beach relaxation.

Following Prison Island, you will embark on a trip to the Spice Farm for a tour. On this tour, you will see and taste the tropical fruits, spice plantations, or plots on the island. A variety of spice plants used as ornaments, medicines, flowers, fruits, and other interesting flora can be observed during the spice tour. After the tour, the driver will take you back to your hotel.

Day 3: DEPARTURE

Pick up from the hotel and transfer to the airport/ferry for departure.
