Our Mission

Creating Unforgettable Memories in Tanzania

At African Flamingo Tour and Safari, our mission is to provide travelers with the best experience and unforgettable memories in Tanzania's stunning destinations. We are dedicated to ensuring your journey with us is filled with joy, adventure, and cultural enrichment.

Our Office

African Flamingo Tour and Safari Company operates from two offices located in the picturesque Arusha region and the vibrant Zanzibar at Unguja.

Why Choose African Flamingo Tour and Safari?
  • We are a local tour operator with our own offices, highly experienced team, and luxury safari vehicles.
  • Our multilingual safari guides (English, French, Spanish) ensure a personalized and enriching experience.
  • We pride ourselves on dedicated customer service, making every visitor's tour an unforgettable experience.
Guaranteed Security

Your security is our priority. We operate 24/7, 365 days a year, and are fully registered and accredited by the Tanzania National Park Authority (TANAPA).
